Employee Attendance and Surveillance Management for Sonatrach

This desktop application is designed to streamline the management of employees, teams, and surveillance points across various Sonatrach complexes. It provides comprehensive tools for CRUD operations, creating employee cards, and applying advanced filters for efficient data management.

The system also facilitates the management of Sonatrach complexes, allowing for the addition and organization of surveillance points. Users can easily oversee employee teams, with team leaders having the ability to generate surveillance reports and update attendance records by marking employees as present or absent.

This application has significantly improved our ability to manage surveillance operations and employee attendance across multiple complexes. The user-friendly interface and robust features make it an indispensable tool for our daily operations.

Sara Wilsson

Team Leader

The system supports the creation and management of surveillance teams, ensuring that each team operates effectively within their designated areas. With advanced filtering options and a seamless CRUD process, managing users and employees has never been easier.

Team leaders can also utilize the application to produce detailed reports on surveillance activities, ensuring that all operations are documented and any changes to attendance are promptly reflected.
